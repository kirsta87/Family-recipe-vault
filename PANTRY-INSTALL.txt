RECIPE VAULT PANTRY + MEAL PLANNING v130

Upload the entire contents of the Family-recipe-vault folder to GitHub, replacing matching files and adding:
- pantry.html
- pantry.js

What is included
- Separate Pantry page linked from the Vault and Meal Planner.
- Giant voice/type kitchen brain-dump.
- Speech-to-list review before anything is saved.
- Add/update/remove phrases.
- Editable pantry inventory.
- Item types: Use it up, Keep stocked, Pantry staple.
- Preferred stock, normal package size, package unit, store, and brand fields.
- Monthly pantry check-in reminder.
- Meal Planner pantry panel showing:
  * pantry ingredients already used by planned meals
  * perishable items still waiting to be used
  * recipes that use those items
  * keep-stocked rules
- Shopping lists NEVER silently remove pantry items. Matching items remain on the list with a Pantry says note for review.

Important current behavior
- Pantry information is stored in this browser's local storage in this version.
- Chrome/Edge microphone dictation generally works best. Typing/pasting always works.
- Ingredient matching is intentionally conservative and advisory.

Quick test
1. Open Pantry.
2. Say or type: Half a carton of chicken broth, two jars pasta sauce, unopened cream cheese, and throw out the cilantro.
3. Click Turn into a list.
4. Review and Apply these updates.
5. Edit chicken broth: Keep stocked, preferred stock 2, package 32 oz, Walmart, Great Value.
6. Open Meal Planner and confirm the Pantry + This Week panel appears.
7. Generate a shopping list and confirm pantry-matched ingredients stay on the list with a note.
